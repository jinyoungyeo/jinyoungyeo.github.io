I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Yonsei University. My research focuses on large language models, AI agents, human–AI collaboration, conversational intelligence, and medical AI.

Before joining Yonsei University, I worked at SKT Brain. I received my Ph.D. from POSTECH and completed research internships at Adobe Research.

Research

Language Models

Reasoning, dialogue, memory, personalization, and robust evaluation.

AI Agents

Long-horizon agents, world models, tool use, and interactive learning.

Human–AI Collaboration

Collaborative decision making, behavior alignment, and clinical agents.

Medical AI

Multimodal clinical reasoning, ophthalmic imaging, and decision support.
